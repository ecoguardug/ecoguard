About EcoGUARD Initiative Uganda

EcoGUARD Initiative Uganda is a youth-led environmental movement founded by passionate engineering students committed to protecting nature and building a sustainable future. We believe that the health of our environment is directly linked to the well-being of our communities and future generations. By combining science, technology, and grassroots action, we address critical challenges such as climate change, deforestation, waste pollution, water scarcity, and poor sanitation across Uganda. Through innovative engineering solutions, community engagement, and environmental education, we empower young people and local communities to become active stewards of the environment. Our work ranges from tree planting and clean-up campaigns to renewable energy projects, eco-innovation labs, and green entrepreneurship programs.
