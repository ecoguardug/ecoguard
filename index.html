Protecting Nature. Empowering Youth. Engineering a Greener Uganda.

EcoGUARD Initiative Uganda is a youth-led environmental movement driven by passionate engineering students and community leaders working to safeguard our natural resources. Through science, innovation, and grassroots action, we tackle climate change, restore ecosystems, and promote sustainable practices. Together, we are shaping a resilient future where people and nature thrive in harmony.
