About EcoGUARD

Building Uganda's Green Future With Youth Leadership

Who We Are

EcoGUARD Initiative Uganda is a youth-led movement founded by engineering students and community leaders. We work at the intersection of climate action, social impact, and practical innovation.

Our model combines hands-on field work, education, and locally adaptable technologies that improve environmental resilience for schools, households, and communities.

Core Values

Integrity

Transparent work and accountable partnerships.

Innovation

Engineering ideas that solve real local challenges.

Inclusion

Youth, women, and communities at the center.

Sustainability

Long-term environmental and social outcomes.
